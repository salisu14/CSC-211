About the College

The Anchau College of Information Technology received its charter, along with two other newly established, from the Kaduna State Government of Nigeria on February 9, 2021. The College campus is located in the Kubau LG Headquarter Anchau of Kaduna state, Anchau, an ancient town in the north western Nigeria. The College host town, Anchau, offers the charm of a small agricultural town, with easy access to major metropolitan areas such as Ikara, Lere,Soba and Tudun wada Kano. The College recognizes the importance of its location in Anchau and seeks to link its research and teaching to the vast resources of these great metropolitan areas in the surrounding. ANCITECH seeks to attract a diverse and international Schools and student body, to support research and teaching on global issues, and to create academic relationships with many College and higher educational institutions in Nigeria and across the world. It expects all areas of the College to advance knowledge and learning at the highest level and to covey the products of its efforts to the community, the nation, and the world.
